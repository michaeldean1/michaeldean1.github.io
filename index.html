The Lifecycle of Your Website

Your website is created from a 5-stage process, that starts with concept and wireframes and ends with a live demonstration.

Wireframes and Fonts

This stage is where you choose the layout of your website pages. You are given free reign and may choose or combine any styles you wish.

It is also at this point that you select what fonts your site will have. You may choose any number of fonts that I have readily available. Just ask for the font list.

Colour Scheme and Logo

At this time you choose the colour palette, or you can supply me with a company logo and have me decide on an appropriate colour scheme.

If you do not have a logo and want me to design one for you, I can do that for you as well.

Page MockUps

When you are satisfied with a colour scheme, typography and logo you are presented with page mockups.

These designs bring all your choices together, allowing you to see what your site will look like when completed.

Images and Function

If you have any images that you want displayed, just send them to me.

Or if you would prefer I can find suitable images, that are free for commercial use.

It is also at this time that I create the functionality for your site. Ensuring that it does exactly what you want it to.

Site Demonstration

When the site is completed it is demonstrated for 72 hours.

It is at this moment that you have the freedom to explore your site, and bring to my attention anything that you would like to add.
